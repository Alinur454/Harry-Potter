The Film Series

8 Films
2001-2011 Released
$7.7B Box Office

The Harry Potter film series brought J.K. Rowling's magical world to life on the big screen, spanning a decade of filmmaking. The final book was adapted into two films, allowing for a more complete telling of the epic conclusion.

From Daniel Radcliffe, Emma Watson, and Rupert Grint's debut as children to their coming-of-age performances, the films captured the essence of growing up in the wizarding world.

A Magical Legacy

Cultural Impact

Harry Potter has become a global phenomenon, inspiring theme parks, fan communities, and countless works of fan fiction.

Literary Achievement

The series has been translated into over 80 languages and has sold more than 500 million copies worldwide.

Educational Value

The books have encouraged millions of children to read and explore themes of friendship, courage, and moral choice.
