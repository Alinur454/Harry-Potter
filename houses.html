The Four Houses of Hogwarts

"You might belong in Gryffindor, where dwell the brave at heart..."

Gryffindor

"Courage, Nerve, and Chivalry"

Founder: Godric Gryffindor
Element: Fire
Colors: Scarlet and Gold
Head of House: Professor McGonagall

Gryffindor values courage, bravery, nerve, and chivalry. Its members are typically brave, daring, and willing to stand up for what's right, even in the face of adversity.

Notable Members:

  • Harry Potter
  • Hermione Granger
  • Ron Weasley
  • Albus Dumbledore

Slytherin

"Cunning, Ambition, and Resourcefulness"

Founder: Salazar Slytherin
Element: Water
Colors: Green and Silver
Head of House: Professor Snape

Slytherin values ambition, cunning, leadership, and resourcefulness. Members are often determined to achieve their goals and are natural leaders.

Notable Members:

  • Severus Snape
  • Draco Malfoy
  • Tom Riddle
  • Merlin

Ravenclaw

"Wit, Learning, and Wisdom"

Founder: Rowena Ravenclaw
Element: Air
Colors: Blue and Bronze
Head of House: Professor Flitwick

Ravenclaw values intelligence, knowledge, wit, and learning. Members are typically creative, clever, and love to learn new things.

Notable Members:

  • Luna Lovegood
  • Cho Chang
  • Padma Patil
  • Garrick Ollivander

Hufflepuff

"Hard Work, Patience, and Loyalty"

Founder: Helga Hufflepuff
Element: Earth
Colors: Yellow and Black
Head of House: Professor Sprout

Hufflepuff values hard work, patience, loyalty, and fair play. Members are typically kind, tolerant, and unafraid of toil.

Notable Members:

  • Cedric Diggory
  • Nymphadora Tonks
  • Newt Scamander
  • Fat Friar
